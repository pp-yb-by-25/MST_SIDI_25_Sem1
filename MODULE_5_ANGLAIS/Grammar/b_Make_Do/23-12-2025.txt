Exercice : Complétez les phrases avec "make" ou "do"

I always ___do___ my homework after school.

She wants to ___make___ a cake for her friend’s birthday.

Can you ___do___ me a favor?

He is going to ___do___ the dishes after dinner.

We need to ___make___ a decision about the trip.

My brother often ___makes___ mistakes in his English exercises.

They are going to ___make___ a phone call to the company.

I must ___do___ my best in the exam tomorrow.

She loves to ___make___ crafts in her free time.

He promised to __do____ the shopping today.
